faq

Can I take pictures during the ceremony?

We would love for this to be an “unplugged wedding" where everyone is completely present for the ceremony. Therefore, we kindly request that guests don’t take any photos or videos during the ceremony. As much as we love that you are as excited as we are to capture our big day, guests having their phones out ends up causing the professional photos to show little phones all around, rather than capturing your smiling faces. We have picked exceptional photographers and videographers, and we promise to share the pictures as soon as we receive them. ☺

Are kids invited?

Unfortunately, we can only accommodate a few close family children. In order to allow everyone, including parents, a weekend of relaxation, we have chosen for our wedding weekend to be an (almost) adult-only occasion. We hope this advance notice means you are still able to share our big day and will enjoy having the weekend off!

What's the weather going to be like?

We’re anticipating low to mid 80s with mild humidity!
