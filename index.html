plan your trip

traveling to cabo

Flights

Los Cabos International Airport (SJD) is the airport to fly into!
The Sheraton Grand Los Cabos (our recommended hotel) is approximately 25 minutes away from the SJD airport.

Getting from the airport to your hotel

Impala Ground Services is a private transportation service that's offering a discount to our wedding guests. You can set up your ride before you get to Mexico. To do that, please use this link and enter the username and password below. You can add your detailed flight information here to find other guests to share rides. Once you receive an email confirmation, you’re all set!

Username: X
Password: X

Another option is taxis or Uber (which is much cheaper in Mexico). There are plenty of taxis waiting outside of the airport. Most cab drivers only take cash (USD or pesos), so make sure you have some on you before starting the trip.

Room Blocks

The Sheraton Grand Los Cabos Hacienda Del Mar is our preferred hotel where we plan to stay and we have reserved a block of rooms for our guests. When booking, please use this link and book before February 25, 2021 to get our discounted rates.

You can also choose to call or email the hotel to book but make sure to request the special “Huang and Weston Wedding” rates.


the wedding day

ceremony & reception location

Our wedding is at a beautiful outdoor event space called Acre, a 25 piece of farmland adjacent to San Jose del Cabo and located about 30 minutes from our suggested hotel.

Getting to Acre

We will have private vans transporting guests to and from Acre from the Sheraton Grand Los Cabos. If you’re staying at another hotel, chances are they can hail you a cab to either our pick-up location or the venue. If you’re staying at an Airbnb, Cabo also has Uber!
